Q1: Why might we consider using git?

Answer: Git is an effective and widespread version control system used to facilitate collaboration on software development. 

Q2: What field could data science be applied to? Give an example and how it might help

Answer: Data science could be applied to healthcare, such as by analyzing the efficicy of a certain cancer treatment across US hospitals to improve treatment.

Q3: What is the difference between merging and rebasing?

Answer: Merging incorporates new commits non-destructively, while rebasing incorporates new commits destructively.
